# Validation Plan

Do not keep adding features before testing.

## Goal

Find out whether people actually want LearnSphere.

## Test with 20 users

Target groups:

1. Engineering students
2. Medical/science students
3. Readers who search novels
4. Researchers
5. Teachers/tutors

## Tasks to give users

Do not ask: "Do you like it?"

Ask them to complete tasks:

1. Find a textbook you need.
2. Find a public-domain novel.
3. Search a topic you are studying.
4. Use Student Mode.
5. Request a missing item.

## Observe

Record:

- What they search
- What fails
- Where they get confused
- Which source they trust
- Whether they click WorldCat/Open Library/Google Books
- Whether they understand the AI answer panel

## Success signal

If 5 out of 20 users say they would use it weekly, continue.

## Next feature decision

Choose only one path:

1. Student learning graph
2. Library/book availability finder
3. Research knowledge graph

Do not build all three equally at the beginning.
